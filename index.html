Chicken butter masala

Momos

Cake

  • 2 cup all purpose flour
  • 100 ml tomato ketchup
  • 1 tomato
  • 2 onion
  • 1 teaspoon chilli flakes
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 teaspoon virgin olive oil
  • 100 gm processed cheese
  • 4 mushrooms
  • 1/2 capsicum (green pepper)
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella
  • 1 tablespoon dry yeast
  • water as required

Step 1 Prepare the pizza dough

To prepare the pizza dough, start by combining all-purpose flour with salt and baking powder on a kneading plate. Create a well in the center and add olive oil. Separately, activate yeast by mixing it with warm water and sugar, allowing it to rise for 10-15 minutes. Combine the risen yeast with the flour mixture, knead into a dough using water, and let it rest for 4-6 hours. Knead the dough again before use, resulting in a ready-to-use pizza dough.

Step 2 Prepare the pizza base

Preheat the oven at 180 degree Celsius. Now, is the time to make the pizza base when the dough is ready. Dust the space a little using dry flour and take a large amount of the pizza dough. Using a rolling pin, roll this dough into a nice circular base. (Note: Make sure that the circular base is even at all ends.) Once you have made the base, use a fork and prick the base with it so that the base doesn't rise and gets baked nicely. Put it into the preheated oven and bake it 10 minutes. Now, your pizza base is ready.

Step 3 Chop all the vegetables for the pizza

Now, wash the capsicum and slice it thinly in a bowl. Then, peel the onions and cut thin slices of it as well in another bowl. And finally, cut tomatoes and mushrooms in the same manner. However, make sure that those tomatoes have less juice in them. Once all the veggies are done, Now, grate the processed and mozzarella cheese in separate bowls.

Step 4 Spread the sauce and veggies on the base

Then, take the fresh pizza base and apply tomato ketchup all over. Spread half the processed cheese all over the base and evenly put the veggies all across the base. Once you have put all the veggies, put a thick layer of mozzarella cheese.

Step 5 Bake the pizza at 250 degree Celsius for 10 minutes

Put this pizza base in a baking tray and place it inside the oven. Let the pizza bake 10 minutes at 250 degree Celsius. Once done, take out the baking tray and slice the pizza. Sprinkle oregano and chilli flakes as per your taste and serve hot. (Note: Make sure that the oven is preheated at 250 degree Celsius for 5 minutes at least.)

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • cup mixed vegetables (e.g., carrots, peas, green beans)
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste (or minced ginger and garlic)
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon biryani masala (or garam masala)
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil or ghee
  • Fresh cilantro (coriander leaves) for garnish (optional)

Step 1 Cook Basmati Rice

Rinse 1 cup of basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Soak the rice in water for 30 minutes, then drain. In a pot, bring 2 cups of water to a boil. Add the drained rice, a pinch of salt, and cook until the rice is 70-80% done (partially cooked). Drain the rice and set aside.

Step 2 Prepare Vegetable Mixture

Heat vegetable oil or ghee in a pan over medium heat. Add thinly sliced onions and sauté until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another minute until fragrant. Add mixed vegetables and chopped tomatoes to the pan. Stir in biryani masala (or garam masala), turmeric powder, and salt to taste. Cook the vegetables until they are tender but still slightly crunchy. Remove from heat.

Step 3 Layer and Cook Biryani

In the same pot used for cooking rice, spread half of the partially cooked rice in a layer. Spread the cooked vegetable mixture evenly over the rice layer. Top with the remaining rice to form the final layer. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and cook the biryani over low heat for 10-12 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

Step 4 Serve and Garnish

Once done, gently fluff the biryani with a fork to mix the layers. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves (coriander) for added freshness and flavor. Serve hot with raita (yogurt dip), pickle, or salad on the side.

  • 200 grams egg noodles (or any noodles of your choice)
  • 200 grams boneless chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 bell pepper (capsicum), thinly sliced
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped (adjust to taste)
  • 3-4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili sauce (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro (coriander leaves) for garnish (optional)

Step 1 Cook the Noodles

Bring a pot of water to a boil and cook the egg noodles according to package instructions until they are al dente. Drain the cooked noodles, rinse them under cold water, and set them aside.

Step 2 Stir-Fry Chicken and Vegetables

Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add minced garlic and chopped green chilies. Sauté for a few seconds until fragrant. Add thinly sliced onions and bell peppers to the pan. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until they start to soften. Push the vegetables to one side of the pan and add the sliced chicken breast to the empty side. Cook the chicken for 3-4 minutes until it's no longer pink and starts to brown.

Step 3 Combine Noodles and Sauce

Lower the heat to medium and add the cooked noodles to the pan. Pour soy sauce, chili sauce, and vinegar over the noodles and chicken. Toss everything together gently using tongs or a spatula to coat the noodles and chicken evenly with the sauce. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the amount of chili sauce according to your spice preference.

Step 4 Serve Chicken Hakka Noodles

Remove the pan from heat once the noodles are heated through and well-coated with the sauce. Transfer the chicken hakka noodles to serving plates. Garnish with fresh cilantro (coriander leaves) for added freshness and flavor. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade chicken hakka noodles!

  • 500 grams boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste (or minced ginger and garlic)
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream or full-fat yogurt
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro (coriander leaves) for garnish (optional)

Step 1 Cook the Chicken

Heat 1 tablespoon of butter in a pan over medium-high heat. Add the chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another minute until fragrant. Add the chicken pieces to the pan and cook until they are lightly browned on all sides.

Step 2 Prepare the Masala Sauce

Push the chicken to one side of the pan and add the remaining 1 tablespoon of butter to the empty space. Once the butter melts, add the red chili powder, turmeric powder, and garam masala to the butter. Stir for a few seconds until fragrant. Pour in the pureed tomatoes and mix well with the spices and chicken. Season with salt to taste.

Step 3 Simmer and Finish

Lower the heat to medium-low, cover the pan, and let the chicken cook in the tomato gravy for 10-12 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and tender. Stir in the heavy cream or full-fat yogurt and simmer for an additional 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.

Step 4 Serve Chicken Butter Masala

Garnish with fresh cilantro (coriander leaves) for added freshness and flavor. Serve hot with steamed rice, naan, or roti.

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • Water, as needed
  • Salt, a pinch
  • 1 cup finely chopped mixed vegetables (cabbage, carrots, bell peppers, etc.)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated or minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil (vegetable oil or any neutral oil)
  • 1 teaspoon chili sauce (adjust to taste)

Step 1 Prepare Momos Dough

In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour and a pinch of salt. Gradually add water, a little at a time, and knead to form a smooth and firm dough. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for 30 minutes.

Step 2 Make Vegetable Filling

Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add minced garlic and ginger, sauté for a few seconds until fragrant. Add finely chopped onions and cook until they turn translucent. Add the chopped mixed vegetables (cabbage, carrots, bell peppers, etc.) and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until they are slightly cooked but still crunchy. Season with soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix well and cook for another minute. Remove from heat and let the filling cool completely.

Step 3 Shape and Steam Momos

Divide the rested dough into small lemon-sized balls. Roll out each ball into a thin circle (about 3-4 inches in diameter) using a rolling pin. Place a spoonful of the vegetable filling in the center of each dough circle. Fold the edges of the dough circle towards the center, pleating and sealing them to form a momo shape (you can refer to online tutorials for different folding techniques). Repeat with the remaining dough and filling to make all the momos.

Step 4 Steam Momos

Grease a steamer basket or idli plates with oil to prevent sticking. Arrange the shaped momos in the steamer basket, leaving some space between them. Steam the momos over boiling water for 12-15 minutes or until the dough becomes translucent and cooked. Remove the steamed momos from the steamer and keep them warm.

Step 5 Serve Momos

Serve the hot and steamy momos immediately with the dipping sauce on the side. Enjoy these delicious vegetable momos as a snack or appetizer!

  • 1.5 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 cup plain yogurt (curd)
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil or melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (for flavor)

Step 1 Preheat Oven and Prepare Cake Pan

Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease a round cake pan (8-inch diameter) with butter or oil, and dust it lightly with flour to prevent sticking.

Step 2 Mix Dry Ingredients

In a mixing bowl, sift together the all-purpose flour and baking powder. Add powdered sugar to the flour mixture and mix well to combine.

Step 3 Combine Wet Ingredients

In another bowl, whisk together the plain yogurt (curd) and vegetable oil (or melted butter) until smooth. If desired, add vanilla extract to the wet mixture for flavor.

Step 4 Combine Dry and Wet Ingredients

Gradually add the dry flour mixture to the wet yogurt mixture. Use a spatula or whisk to gently fold the ingredients together until you get a smooth cake batter. Avoid overmixing.

Step 5 Bake the Cake and Serve

Pour the cake batter into the prepared cake pan, spreading it evenly. Tap the pan gently on the counter to remove any air bubbles. Place the cake pan in the preheated oven and bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. The baking time may vary depending on your oven, so keep an eye on the cake after 25 minutes. Once the cake is baked, remove it from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes. After cooling, gently run a knife around the edges of the cake to loosen it from the pan. Invert the cake onto a wire rack and let it cool completely before slicing and serving.
